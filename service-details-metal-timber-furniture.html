What is metal-timber furniture?

Metal-timber furniture is a type of furniture that is made from a combination of metal and timber. This combination of materials can create furniture that is both strong and durable, as well as stylish and modern. Metal-timber furniture can be used in a variety of settings, including homes, offices, restaurants, and cafes. It is particularly well-suited for areas that receive heavy use, such as kitchens and dining rooms.
