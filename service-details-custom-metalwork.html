What do we mean by custom metalwork?

We build metal work to your custom order, from artistic pieces all the way to furniture with unique flare. We only need to book a short meeting with you to discuss exactly what you need and we can design it, agree on it and then build it!

What makes us qualified to do your custom metal work?

At Simbi Smith, we believe that custom metalwork is an art form. We take great pride in our ability to create unique and high-quality metal products that meet the specific needs of our customers.
Here are just a few of the reasons why we are the best for custom metalwork:

  • Experience and expertise: We have over 5 years of experience in the custom metalwork industry. Our team of skilled and experienced fabricators is capable of handling even the most complex and challenging projects.
  • State-of-the-art equipment: We use the latest and greatest metalworking equipment to ensure that our products are made to the highest standards of quality.
  • Commitment to customer satisfaction: We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience. We work closely with our customers to understand their needs and ensure that their expectations are met or exceeded.

In addition to the above, here are some other specific benefits of working with Simbi Smith for custom metalwork:

  • Wide range of services: We offer a wide range of custom metalwork services, including cutting, bending, welding, and finishing. We can also provide custom design services to help you bring your ideas to life.
  • Competitive prices: We offer competitive prices on all of our custom metalwork services. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible value for their money.
  • Fast turnaround times: We understand that our customers need their custom metalwork products quickly. We strive to provide fast turnaround times on all of our projects.

If you are looking for a custom metalwork company that can provide you with the highest quality products and services, then Simbi Smith is the right choice for you. Contact us today for a free consultation.
