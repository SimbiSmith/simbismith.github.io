What is fabrication?

Fabrication is the process of creating products or structures by combining raw materials or semi-finished materials. It involves a variety of processes, such as cutting, bending, welding, and assembling. Fabrication can be used to create a wide range of products, from simple brackets to complex machines and equipment.
