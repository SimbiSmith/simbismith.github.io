Our Story

What started a simple hobby in the midst of COVID-19 Lockdowns, slowly emerged into a thriving business. With nothing but an old welding machine inherited from my grandfather, I honed my skills day and night, becoming more and more proficient as the time went on. I roped in the assistance of Matthew, my younger brother, after he left high school. He is now creating his own legacy as we work on more and more contract. It is amazing what dedication and hard work can do your the mind, body and soul. We define our work on 3 things:

  • Dedication to perfection
  • Dedication to our clients
  • Dedication to delievering our services as quickly as we can
