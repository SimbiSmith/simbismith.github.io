What is 3D rendering and animation?

3D rendering is the process of creating a 2D image from a 3D model. It is a computer graphics process that involves using mathematical calculations to generate images that appear to be three-dimensional. 3D rendering can be used to create a wide range of images, from photorealistic images to stylized images.

Why should you use 3D rendering to view the products you want?

3D Rendering has a multitude of advantages, there are many reasons that you would want to use a 3D image to visualize your end product with us, especially if you are considering having a custom built piece commissioned. We can help you understand what you want well before any fabrication starts with state of the art 3D rendering technology.

  • Photorealism: 3D rendering can be used to create images that are indistinguishable from real photographs. This can be useful for marketing and advertising purposes, as well as for creating realistic architectural visualizations and product designs.
  • Versatility: 3D rendering can be used to create a wide range of images, from photorealistic images to stylized images. This makes it a versatile tool that can be used for a variety of purposes.
  • Accuracy: 3D rendering can be used to create accurate representations of real-world objects and environments. This can be useful for engineering and design purposes, as well as for creating realistic architectural visualizations and product designs.
  • Communication: 3D rendering can be used to create images and animations that can help you understand your ideas and designs in a clear and concise way.
  • Time and cost savings: 3D rendering can help you to save time and money by reducing the need to create physical prototypes or to take photographs.

Before Simbi Smith became a steel and metal work company, our employees originally worked in several sectors or art and 3D imaging and design. We are the current industry leading standard for 3D imaging within the Zimbabwean market.
